

## **Final Color Scheme Design**  

### **Body Background**  
- Use **F4F9FF** (a very light, almost white blue tint) for the overall body background to give a clean and modern look.  
- Alternatively, for sections requiring contrast, use **00D4D6** with **10-15% opacity**.  

---

### **Navbar Design**  
1. **Background:**  
   **051C42** (Dark Blue-Gray) for a professional, bold appearance.  
2. **Text & Links:**  
   - Default Text: **FFFFFF** (White).  
   - Active/Hover Links: **00D4D6** (Cyan).  
3. **Icons in Navbar:**  
   Default: **FFFFFF**, Hover: **00D4D6**.  

---

### **Footer Design**  
1. **Background:**  
   **060F2C** (Deep Navy).  
2. **Text & Links:**  
   - General Text: **FFFFFF**.  
   - Links: **00D4D6**, Hover: **0094EB**.  
3. **Icons in Footer:**  
   Default: **00D4D6**, Hover: **0094EB**.  

---

### **Content (Text & Headings)**  
1. **Background of Content Section:**  
   - Use **FFFFFF** (White) for maximum readability.  
2. **Text Colors:**  
   - **Headings:** **0094EB** (Sky Blue).  
   - **Body Text:** **051C42** (Dark Blue-Gray).  
   - **Highlight/Emphasis:** **00B2E1** (Light Blue).  

---

### **Buttons**  
1. **Primary Buttons:**  
   - Background: **0094EB** (Sky Blue).  
   - Text: **FFFFFF**.  
   - Hover Background: **00B2E1**, Text: **FFFFFF**.  
2. **Secondary Buttons:**  
   - Background: **00D4D6** (Cyan).  
   - Text: **051C42**.  
   - Hover Background: **0094EB**, Text: **FFFFFF**.  
3. **Outline Buttons:**  
   - Border: **00B2E1**, Text: **00B2E1**.  
   - Hover Background: **00B2E1**, Text: **FFFFFF**.  

---

### **Icons**  
1. **Default Icon Color:** **00B2E1** (Light Blue).  
2. **Hover Effect:** **00D4D6** (Cyan).  
3. **Icons in Dark Sections:**  
   Default: **FFFFFF**, Hover: **00B2E1**.  

---

### **Cards**  
1. **Card Background:**  
   Use **FFFFFF** for a clean look.  
2. **Card Border:**  
   - **00D4D6** (Cyan) for a subtle and modern outline.  
3. **Card Headings:**  
   - Color: **0094EB**.  
4. **Card Text:**  
   - Use **051C42** for body text.  
5. **Card Buttons (inside cards):**  
   - Same as the button styles mentioned above.  

---

### **Complete Example**  
#### **Body:** Background: **F4F9FF**  
#### **Navbar:** Background: **051C42**, Links: **FFFFFF** (Default), Hover: **00D4D6**  
#### **Footer:** Background: **060F2C**, Links: **00D4D6**, Hover: **0094EB**  
#### **Content:** Background: **FFFFFF**, Headings: **0094EB**, Text: **051C42**, Highlight: **00D4D6**  
#### **Buttons:** Primary: **0094EB** (Background), Text: **FFFFFF**  
#### **Cards:** Background: **FFFFFF**, Border: **00D4D6**, Headings: **0094EB**, Text: **051C42**
